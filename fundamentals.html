What is an ADR?

Architecture Decision Record

Document that captures WHY you made a technical choice

‚ùå Tool-focused
"Learn every feature of Kafka"
‚úÖ Decision-focused
"Why Netflix chose Kafka for 1B+ events/day"

üí° Why This Matters

ADRs are your future self's best friend. When someone asks "why did we use Redis instead of Memcached?" 2 years later, you'll have the answer. Real example: Spotify documented why they chose Cassandra - saved months of debate when scaling from 1M to 100M users.

Section What to Include
Context Problem + Constraints (scale, budget, latency)
Decision What you chose (tech/pattern)
Rationale WHY this choice (numbers + trade-offs)
Consequences ‚úÖ Benefits, ‚ùå Limitations, ‚ö™ Changes
Alternatives What you rejected and why
